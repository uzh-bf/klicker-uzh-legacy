User Settings

Change your user settings on the top right corner, clicking on Settings (1.) There you can change:

  1. your account data, which means

  2. your E-mail address

  3. the participants' link

  4. your institutions, where "migrated" means, that your data from you former Klicker account was migrated

  5. your use case description

  6. your password.
